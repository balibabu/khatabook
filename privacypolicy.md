## 2. Types of Data Collected
### Personal Data
While using Our Service, We ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information to manage your account and store your data. This includes:
*   **Email Address:** Used for account registration and authentication.
*   **Password:** Used for securing your account (encrypted via Firebase Auth).
*   **User-Generated Content:** Any data you create, upload, or modify through CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations within the app. This data is stored securely in **Google Firestore**.

### Usage and Technical Data
Our Service uses Firebase SDKs which may automatically collect certain information, including:
*   Your device's unique ID.
*   Your mobile operating system.
*   Basic diagnostic data to ensures the app works correctly.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
The Company uses your data for the following purposes:
*   **To provide and maintain our Service:** Including allowing you to log in and sync your data across devices.
*   **To manage Your Account:** Your email identifies you as a user and gives you access to your specific data stored in Firestore.
*   **Data Storage:** Your data is stored "in the cloud" via Firebase so that you do not lose it if you change devices.

The security of Your Personal Data is important to Us. We use Firebase's industry-standard encryption to protect your data both "in transit" (from your phone to the server) and "at rest" (stored in the database).
